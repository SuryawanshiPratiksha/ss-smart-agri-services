About Us

Transforming Agriculture with Data-Driven Insights and Drone Solutions

Established in 2021 in Maharashtra, Pune, SS Smart Agri Services is dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture through cutting-edge drone technology. We specialize in providing agricultural solutions that boost productivity, reduce costs, and improve crop health.

We leverage drone technology for a variety of applications, including crop monitoring, precision spraying, and data analytics. Our mission is to create sustainable and efficient farming practices that cater to the growing demands of the agricultural industry.

We Provide:

  • Faster coverage
  • Lower labor costs
  • Less pesticide usage
  • Improved crop yields
  • Enhanced crop monitoring

10-Liter Agricultural Spraying Drone Overview

Spraying Capacity

The "10 liters" refers to the drone's tank capacity, meaning it can carry up to 10 liters of liquid (such as pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, or fertilizers) for spraying over crops.

Key Features

  • Spray System: Multiple nozzles and pump system
  • GPS Navigation: Automated flight paths
  • Multispectral & Thermal Cameras: Crop health monitoring

Advantages

  • Efficiency: Reduces labor and time
  • Precision: Accurate spraying with minimal waste
  • Cost Savings: Long-term savings in chemicals and labor
  • Safety: Reduces exposure to hazardous chemicals

Flight Time

Typically, these drones have a flight time of 20 to 30 minutes per charge, depending on factors like weather, terrain, and the weight of the tank.

Applications

  • Crop Protection: Pesticides and fungicides spraying
  • Fertilization: Precise fertilizer application
  • Watering: Irrigation in dry areas

Drone Design

These drones are designed with weather-resistant frames and autonomous flight modes for ease of operation and high durability.

Cost

The price varies between $3,000 to $20,000 depending on features such as GPS systems, camera sensors, and battery capacity.

Regulations

Compliance with local aviation regulations is essential, including flight altitude limits and operating within visual line-of-sight.

Maintenance

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the spraying system, checking for wear on the propellers, and updating flight software.

Autonomous Flight Systems

Autonomous flight systems improve the efficiency of spraying operations through GPS, AI, and machine learning. Drones can navigate pre-programmed flight paths, ensuring precise spraying and adapting to changing environments for optimal results.
